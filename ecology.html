Analysis Methods & Technologies

Advanced scientific methods and technologies used to analyze environmental indicators.

Spectroscopy

Measures light absorption to identify chemical composition in air, water, and soil samples.

Chromatography

Separates and identifies complex mixtures of pollutants and chemicals.

Remote Sensing

Uses satellite data to monitor deforestation, urban expansion, and climate changes.

Biological Assays

Tests toxicity levels and bioaccumulation in living organisms.
